How do I connect to a TV?

1. Make sure to check if your device and TV are on the same Wi-Fi network. Also check if you’re not using VPN, proxies etc.

2. Tap the TV icon at the top right corner in order to establish connection.

3. After the search is complete, select your TV from the list.

4. Complete all necessary steps on your TV.

If you still can’t connect or find your TV, make sure to do the following:

1. Go to Settings on your device.

2. Tap Privacy > Local network.

3. Turn on local network access for the app if it’s turned off.

Here’s the list of supported TVs: Samsung, LG, Roku, Fire TV, Chromecast.

How to start mirroring or casting?

Once you’re connect to the TV, you can start mirroring your screen or cast content such as photos, videos, YouTube and more. Follow the steps below to get started:

1. Make sure you’re connected to TV.

2. Choose what you want to mirror or cast: photos, videos, YouTube, online content, IPTV or your phone screen.

3. Tap the corresponding tile to start.

4. If you want to mirror your phone screen, just tap Screen Mirroring and adjust quality and other parameters.

Why I don’t see my device in the list?

Always make sure that your phone and TV are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Also check the list of supported TVs: Samsung, LG, Roku, Fire TV, Chromecast.

How to manage my subscription?

1. Open the Settings app on your device.

2. Tap your name at the top.

3. Tap Subscriptions.

4. Tap the subscription that you want to manage.
